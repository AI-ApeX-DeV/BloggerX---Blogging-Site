About TCET Open Source

TCET Open Source is an organization where software is not just a commodity to be bought and sold, but a collaborative effort that brings people together to solve problems and build something greater than themselves21.

We are a group of passionate students and faculty members who believe in the power and potential of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) to transform software engineering education and development in TCET.

We work on various projects that aim to enhance the college infrastructure, promote Linux culture, automate tedious tasks, and provide useful tools for students and teachers.

Some of our projects are:

  • TCET Linux: An Arch Linux based Linux distribution customized for TCET labs and classrooms. It comes with pre-installed software packages, themes, settings, and apps that make learning and teaching software engineering easier and more fun.
  • Activity Dekho: A web app that allows you to calculate your AICTE 100 points easily and efficiently. It also helps you create your AICTE diary with all the details of your events and activities.
  • Resume Screener: A web app that uses artificial intelligence to analyse your resume and give you feedback on how to improve it. It also compares your resume with the requirements of FAANGM companies and gives you tips on how to land your dream job.
  • TNP Website: The official website of TCET Training and Placement Cell. It showcases the achievements, opportunities, and events of the TNP cell. It also provides a platform for students to register for placements, upload their resumes, and view their status.
  • Fill My Cycle: A web app that helps you fill cycles in lab evaluation sheet quickly and accurately. It also generates a PDF file of your filled sheet that you can print or share.

We are always looking for new ideas, challenges, and collaborators. If you are interested in joining us or contributing to our projects, please visit our GitHub page or contact us at opensource@tcetmumbai.in.
